You’ll never watch HBO
the same way again.

In fact, you’ll never watch TV the same way again. Subscribe to HBO right from your Apple TV or iOS device — no cable or satellite subscription required. With HBO NOW, you get instant access to every episode of every season of HBO’s award-winning original shows and Hollywood blockbusters. HBO NOW will be available for just $14.99 a month — with no long-term contract or commitment. Sign up on your Apple TV, iPad, or iPhone and get the first month for free.

Watch HBO NOW on the big screen.

With HBO NOW on Apple TV, you’ll catch every sword fight, laugh, and plot twist in its full HD glory. Get hooked on HBO originals including Game of Thrones, Silicon Valley, and Veep — including bonus features and behind-the-scenes extras. You can even watch big premieres the same time they air on TV. With HBO mini-series, documentaries, comedy specials, sports, and hundreds of movies available at any time, you’ll always find something to watch.

Watch HBO NOW anywhere.

With your HBO NOW subscription, you can also enjoy HBO on your iPad, iPhone, or iPod touch. Download the app from the App Store and start streaming your favorite shows or movies from anywhere. Now you’ll never miss a single episode or big season premiere.
